We Buy, Consign, and Recycle Servers

ServerPartsFast is an independent distributor of servers and components located in Atlanta, Georgia. We buy servers and enterprise equipment that is surplus, off lease, or otherwise not needed. We purchase both new and old equipment. We recycle the older equipment and auction off or list any working components that retain value. We specialize in Dell and HP servers and offer economical and evironment-friendly recycling services. If you have excess equipment in bulk please contact us for more information.

Our Promise

We will conduct business with our customers, suppliers and employees in the easiest, most hassle-free way possible. Our goal is 100% satisfaction and a 5 Star rating. We leave positive feedback for all buyers, once you have paid for your item in full. If you are not satisfied with any part of your experience with us, please give us a chance to remedy the situation. We ask that you do not leave us negative/neutral feedback without communicating with us beforehand. Give us a chance to provide a solution and keep you as a happy customer.

Consignment

We are experienced eBay sellers and will be more than happy to consign your servers and networking equipment on eBay. Contact us if you are interested in this service.

We're also consumers!

Those of us at ServerPartsFast are also consumers. We understand you want your purchase as soon as possible. Keeping this in mind, we do our best to ship all orders within one business day.

We ship within the US only, but we also participate in Ebay's Global Shipping Program. If your country is on the list, chances are we can ship to you.

We offer emergency overnight shipping for most of our items, if you'd like this please message us to arrange this. We have inventory beyond what is listed, feel free to ask about other parts.

 

We know you would rather have your purchase sooner than later so we ship as fast as possible. We try our best to ship most of our packages by FedEx Ground the same business day if checkout is completed by 2:00PM EST. On many items we offer multiple shipping options in addition to the standard ground shipping. Click on the eBay shipping tab at the top of this listing for other shipping options.

Emergency Overnight Service:  Contact us if you need this service. Overnight shipping can be expensive, and we understand the urgency when a production server is down.

Alaska, Hawaii, PR, Canada Shipping: The shipping prices listed are for the 48 contiguous states. If you want the product to be shipped to Alaska, Hawaii please request an invoice as the flat shipping fee does not apply for these areas. We will search for the cheapest shipping to your area. As a general rule we do not ship to Canada but we do make exceptions if the customer is willing to schedule the shipping and handle the customs paperwork.

 

 

 

Refunds & Returns

Here at ServerPartsFast we understand the frustration of a product not functioning properly. If you receive a product like this, please contact us through Ebay and allow us to respond in as timely a manner as possible.

We have an excellent eBay rating and will always stand behind our products. All RMA items must be returned in their original condition and must include all items including the packaging. If the returned item has additional damage(dented, chipped, cracked, etc.) or is missing items, a fair estimated value will be returned.

We reserve the right to replace a dysfunctional item first. If we are unable to replace it a refund for your purchase will be issued.

A 20% restocking fee applies to returns due to customer errors or items returned that are tested and working appropriately. Returned shipments must include tracking on the shipment.
